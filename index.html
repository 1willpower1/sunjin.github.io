About Me

I was born in August 1985 in Chizhou, Anhui Province, China. I hold a Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering and currently serve as an Associate Professor at the School of Chemical Engineering, Guangdong University of Petrochemical Technology. I am also a graduate supervisor in the School of Energy and Power Engineering.

I am a core instructor for two National First-Class Undergraduate Courses: Petroleum Refining Engineering and Petrochemical Process Engineering. I have extensive experience in engineering education reform, process simulation, and industryeducation integration.
